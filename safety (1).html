Safety, Rights & Defense

Women's Rights

Women have the right to live free from violence, discrimination, and fear. EmpowerHer provides resources to educate women about their rights and the legal protections available to them.

Toll-Free Helpline Numbers

>

For any emergency or support, women can call the toll-free helpline numbers:
Women Helpline: 1091
Police: 100
Emergency: 112

Kavalan App

>

The Kavalan SOS app, launched by the Tamil Nadu Police, allows women to send immediate alerts to the police in case of emergencies. The app helps in ensuring the safety and security of women.

Self-Defense Training

We organize self-defense training sessions for women to equip them with the skills and confidence to protect themselves in any situation. These sessions are conducted by experienced trainers.
